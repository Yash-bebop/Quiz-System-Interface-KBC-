👑 Round 3 — The Final

Direct & Pass — Champions Decide

Reserved for Top 5 teams · Every team gets equal turns · High risk, high reward

🔁 The Direct & Pass Flow
  • Direct Question: Every question starts as a "Direct" for one specific team — they have the first right to answer.
  • Rotation: Q1 → Team 1 Direct · Q2 → Team 2 Direct · continues fairly for all 5 teams.
  • Discussion Time: The Direct team gets 20 seconds to discuss and give an answer.
  • Passing: If the Direct team says "Pass", the question moves to the next team in alphabetical order.
  • Pass Time: The receiving team gets only 5 seconds to answer or pass again.
  • End Condition: A question ends once someone answers (correctly or wrongly), or if it passes through all 5 teams unanswered.
🏆 Scoring — Round 3
+10 Correct answer on your Direct Question (your own assigned question).
0 Wrong answer on your Direct Question — no penalty for failing your own question.
+5 Correct answer on a Passed Question (half points — you had more time to think).
−2 Wrong answer on a Passed Question — penalty applies for stealing and failing.
