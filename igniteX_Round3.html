👑 Round 3 — The Final

Direct & Pass — MCQ Edition

6 Teams · 5 Questions per Team · KBC-style Lock-in · High risk, high reward

📋 Round Format
  • Each question is assigned as a Direct for one specific team — they lock in their answer by clicking an option.
  • Once locked (turns yellow), the host presses → to reveal: green = correct, red = wrong.
  • If the direct team wants to Pass, they do not click — host moves to the next team.
  • Passing teams get 10 seconds to click an option and lock in, or pass again.
  • A question ends once any team locks in, or all teams pass.
🏆 Scoring
+10 Correct on your own Direct Question.
0 Wrong on your Direct Question — no penalty.
+5 Correct on a Passed Question.
−2 Wrong on a Passed Question.
